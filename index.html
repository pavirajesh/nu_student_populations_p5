The expanding student population
at Northeastern University

Every year, Northeastern becomes home to more and more students. People from all over flock to this dynamic university system with its multuple campuses in various locations. That growth is exciting and promising but also an important reminder why sustainability is so important.

As student populations grow, more and more resources are needed, from residential and dining services to academic buildings and classroom space. It is important for students to understand just how much those resources will be in demand.

The visualization below lets students explore Northeastern's total student population over the past five years, including breakdowns by college organized from largest to smallest.
